DRONE FI-PRE

Executive summary:

When creating the Drone Fi-pre our main question was, "What if we could prevent wildfires before they start?"

The Drone Fi-Pre is a self-sufficient design to help prevent wildfires in a specific area utilizing infrared cameras and electronic weathering sensors. With wireless communication between the deployed drones, we are able to continuously monitor specific regions to identify potential wildfires and predict its path of travel. Drone Fi-Pre serves as one of the first defenses towards wildfires prevention by aiding and coordinating the user to take action to prevent disasters.


Design and composition

The Drone design is inspired by the DJI MAVIC PRO which contains:

- HD quality camera

- FLIR Duo Pro R- Infrared Camera

The Drone will have a Base and docking station that will be composed of :

The Ambient Weather WS-2902C Smart Weather Station

-Solar panels for energy

-Lithium battery in case of solar power malfunction

-RF tag for Precision landing


Platform architecture

The platform architecture below shows the internet of things diagram for the Drone Fi-Pre. The Internet of Things is an interconnection of devices of everyday objects that are able to send and receive data. In the event that there has been a detection, please refer to the drone in red above, it will relay the information to its the Base/Docking Station. The Station will then send the data to the other drones to locate the coordinates and activate the Ambient Weather Station to measure humidity, temperature, wind speed, and direction. After the proper conditions are met, an AI will process the information and relay it to the user with a suggested best course of action and wildfire data.
